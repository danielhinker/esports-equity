All or Nothing

If the project chooses to utilize all or nothing as their funding strategy, then funds are disbursed to the project owner only if and when that project reaches their minimum goal. If a project chooses this option then we take 5% in addition to a transaction fee of 2.9% + $.30 per transaction.
